What is City Heritage

City hertiage is an initiative from the UNESCO World Heritage department. It is created to bring more awareness and interactivity to the rich history of the cities we live in. By combining information with interactivity, and text with photography, UNESCO hopes to create more interest in history by the public.

About UNESCO

United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation, called UNESCO, is an organisation with peace as goal, achieved by international cooperation on subjects as education, science and culture. The programs executed by UNESCO contribute to achieving the so called SDG's, or sustainable development goals in 2030.
