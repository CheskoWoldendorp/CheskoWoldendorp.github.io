City Heritage

UNESCO City Heritage is an initiative of UNESCO, our goal is to show that the history of the city you live in, is important. We want to show people that history and the importance of maintaining it. That even when years and decades pass, everyone can enjoy it and learn from it.

Are you interested in telling about the history of your city or region? On the participate page you will find all the options in which you can!
