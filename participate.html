Images and text

Using images and text together to convey information is very effective. Even more so when these come from the community itself! City Heritage is active on several social media, when you post or use the hashtag of your city, you have a chance that your piece is highlighted on our site.

Become an ambassador

Are you motivated to tell more people about the rich history of your city or region? Then maybe you are fit to become an ambassador! You can apply by sending a motivation letter to our ambassador department, via ambassadors.cityheritage@unesco.com.
